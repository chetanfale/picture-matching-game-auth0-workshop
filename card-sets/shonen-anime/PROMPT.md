# Shonen Anime Card Set — Gemini Prompt

Use this prompt with Google Gemini to generate the card images for this set.

## Prompt

> Generate 8 individual card images for a picture matching memory game. Each image should be a single iconic character or symbol from classic shonen anime, illustrated in a bold, colorful chibi/super-deformed style against a clean, solid-color background.
>
> Create one image each for:
>
> 1. A spiky-haired boy in an orange gi powering up with energy aura (inspired by Dragon Ball)
> 2. A boy in a straw hat with a big grin and a red vest (inspired by One Piece)
> 3. A blond ninja in an orange jumpsuit with whisker marks (inspired by Naruto)
> 4. A boy with spiky orange hair carrying an oversized sword (inspired by Bleach)
> 5. A boy wearing a red cape with a metal arm giving a determined look (inspired by Fullmetal Alchemist)
> 6. A green-haired boy in a hero costume throwing a powered-up punch (inspired by My Hero Academia)
> 7. A boy with black spiky hair wearing a school uniform channeling dark energy from his hands (inspired by Jujutsu Kaisen)
> 8. A scarred boy in a black cloak holding a grimoire book (inspired by Black Clover)
>
> **Requirements for each image:**
>
> - Square aspect ratio (1:1)
> - Simple, bold illustration style — easily recognizable at small sizes (96×128px card display)
> - Vibrant colors with high contrast
> - Clean solid or simple gradient background (each character gets a different background color)
> - No text or watermarks
> - Save as JPEG
>
> Generate each character as a **separate image file**.
