# Corgi Card Set — Gemini Prompt

Use this prompt with Google Gemini to generate the card images for this set.

## Prompt

> Generate 8 individual card images for a picture matching memory game. Each image should be a cute chibi/cartoon corgi with a unique accessory or trait, illustrated in a bold, colorful style against a clean, solid-color background.
>
> Create one image each for:
>
> 1. A corgi wearing a tiny crown, looking regal on a purple background
> 2. A corgi with sunglasses and a surfboard on a blue background
> 3. A corgi in a chef hat holding a spatula on a red background
> 4. A corgi wearing a space helmet floating among stars on a dark blue background
> 5. A corgi with a wizard hat and wand casting sparkles on a teal background
> 6. A corgi in a raincoat splashing in a puddle on a yellow background
> 7. A corgi wearing headphones and bobbing to music on a pink background
> 8. A corgi in a superhero cape striking a pose on an orange background
>
> **Requirements for each image:**
>
> - Square aspect ratio (1:1)
> - Simple, bold chibi illustration style — easily recognizable at small sizes (96×128px card display)
> - Vibrant colors with high contrast
> - Clean solid or simple gradient background (each corgi gets a different background color)
> - No text or watermarks
> - Save as JPEG
>
> Generate each corgi as a **separate image file**.
